Tredway Riverfront Trail - Amenities Overview



Rest rooms, water: None at the trail heads, An out of order SST along the trail 1.5 miles from Lower Burrell or 1 mile from Garver Ferry Road Trailhead.

Bike shop, rentals: Bike rentals at River Forest Country Club, Gattos in Tarentum

Restaurant, groceries: none along the trail. Along Leechburg Road on the way to the trailhead and in New Kensibton.

Camping, simple lodging: None

Swimming, fishing: There are the remains of several summer camps along the River, which could give you access to the river.

Winter sports: Cross-Country skiing.

Wheelchair access: The trail down from Garver Ferry Road is quite steep dropping 300 feet in 0.4 miles. The main trail is railroad grade with crushed limestone. Gates are at least 3 feet wide.

Summary bike rental at Country Club
